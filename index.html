About Me

In July 2024, I started working as a research associate at the Chair of Software and Systems Engineering (Prof. Alexander Pretschner) at the Technical University of Munich. In my research, I am exploring the potential of Generative AI, particularly Large Language Models (LLMs), for code migration and modernization. I have a master's degree in Software Engineering and Technology from Chalmers University of Technology, Sweden. Before joining the chair, I worked as a Data Engineer at Volvo Group. Additionally, I have gained experience as a student engineer at Volvo Cars, Good Solutions, and APTIV. Outside of work, I enjoy sports such as cricket and volleyball. I also love to travel and discover new destinations. You can check out my Instagram for some travel snapshots.
